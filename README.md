# PDF Signature Stamper

A free, private, browser-based tool to stamp your signature on every page of a PDF — no apps, no uploads, no servers.

---

## How to use

1. Open the tool link in **Safari** (iPhone) or **Chrome** (Android/Desktop)
2. Upload your PDF
3. Draw your signature or upload a signature image
4. Select which pages to sign — all pages, a page range, or specific page numbers
5. Choose the position and size of the signature
6. Tap **Stamp all pages**
7. Tap the download link to save your signed PDF

---

## Privacy

- Your PDF and signature **never leave your device**
- Everything runs locally in your browser
- No data is sent to any server

## Features

- Draw signature directly on screen or upload an image
- Ink colour options
- Sign all pages, a continuous range, or hand-picked pages
- Adjustable signature size and position (top/bottom/left/right/center)
- Output filename includes original filename + `_signed` + date and time in IST (e.g. `Contract_signed_22042026_143025.pdf`)
- Works fully offline once the page is loaded
